about us

Rowena Phillips is the Founder Director of Dominion Phillips Educare pvt Ltd and the Principal Consultant Trainer. She is also a visiting Counselor at IIMN. In her 21 years of experience she has been involved in Human Resource, Operations, Training & Coaching Consulting and the Academics. She specializes in the area of Productivity and Performance Management, Leadership Development, Communication Skills, Emotional Intelligence & Psychological Consulting. She has been associated with organizations like Tech Mahindra Business Service, Wipro BPO, St. Xavier's & Group, and Meghe Group of Engineering Institutions, St Vincent Pallotti College of Engineering & Technology, Anjuman College of Engineering. In her counselling experience she has interacted and inspired more than 10,000 students, parents through her motivational talks and Seminars. Academically, she holds a Masters in Psychology and Masters in Human Resource Management. Professionally she is a Certified Meta-Neuro Linguistic Programming Master Practitioner and a Coach with ISNS, USA. She is also a Certified NLP Communication Coach from Society of NLP, USA.
